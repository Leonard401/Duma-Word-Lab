# Privacy Policy for DUMA

Effective date: April 11, 2026

DUMA ("the app", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data may be collected, how it is used, and what choices you have when using DUMA.

If you have privacy-related questions, you can contact us at: `lvisoiu300@gmail.com`

## 1. What DUMA is

DUMA is a mobile word game. The app does not require you to create an account and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or other directly identifying information in order to play.

## 2. Data stored locally on your device

DUMA stores certain gameplay data locally on your device in order to function properly, including:

- campaign progress
- unlocked levels
- words found in each level
- preferred language
- number of free hints used on the current day

This data is stored locally using standard operating system storage mechanisms. We do not operate our own backend server for gameplay sync, and we do not upload this gameplay data to our own servers.

## 3. Advertising and third-party SDKs

DUMA may display advertising through Google AdMob, including banner ads and rewarded ads. When ads are shown, Google and its partners may automatically collect certain technical or usage-related data, depending on your device configuration, Google account settings, and the advertising services available on your device.

This data may include, for example:

- IP address
- device identifiers or advertising identifiers
- diagnostic and performance information
- interactions with ads or with the app
- approximate device and network information

This data may be used for:

- serving and displaying ads
- fraud prevention and invalid traffic detection
- ad performance measurement
- diagnostics and service improvement

For more information about how Google processes data, please review:

- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Google Mobile Ads SDK data disclosure: https://developers.google.com/ad-manager/mobile-ads-sdk/android/privacy/play-data-disclosure

## 4. Consent for ads and privacy choices

In some regions, the law may require user consent before certain types of advertising are shown or before certain advertising identifiers are used. If and when we implement or display a consent flow in the app, your choices will be presented in-app as required by applicable law.

You may also be able to manage advertising settings through your Google account and your device settings, where available.

## 5. Data we do not directly collect

Except for data processed by the advertising providers described above, DUMA does not directly collect:

- your full name
- your email address
- your phone number
- your postal address
- precise location
- contacts
- photos
- microphone input
- camera data
- passwords or login credentials

## 6. Purpose of processing

We use local data only to operate the game, preserve progress, and manage free hints. If ads are enabled, data processed by advertising providers is handled under their own policies for advertising, security, fraud prevention, and measurement purposes.

## 7. Data retention

Data stored locally by DUMA remains on your device until:

- you clear the app's storage from your device settings
- you uninstall the app
- you reinstall the app without restoring local data, if applicable

We do not retain your gameplay progress on our own servers because DUMA does not currently use its own account system or cloud sync backend.

Data collected by Google AdMob or other third-party providers is retained according to their own policies and retention periods.

## 8. Security

We aim to limit data use to what is necessary for the app to function. However, no method of storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

## 9. Your rights

Depending on your country or region, you may have rights regarding access, deletion, objection, or restriction of certain data processing activities. Because DUMA does not currently maintain user accounts or personal profiles on our own servers, most data directly controlled by you can be removed locally by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

For data processed by Google or other third-party providers, please review their own privacy policies and user controls.

## 10. Children

DUMA is not specifically directed to children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that a child has directly provided personal data to us, please contact us using the email above. Since DUMA does not directly collect account or contact information from users, the possibility of direct collection is limited.

## 11. International data transfers

If advertising or other third-party services are used, data may be processed on servers located outside your country of residence, including in jurisdictions where those providers operate.

## 12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect legal, technical, or product changes. Any updated version will include a new effective date.
